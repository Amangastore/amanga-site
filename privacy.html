Privacy Policy

AMANGA (“we”, “our”) operates this website and uses Amazon Selling Partner API (SP‑API) solely to access data related to our own Amazon seller account. We do not sell, lease, or otherwise disclose Amazon Information to third parties.

Information We Access via SP‑API

  • Catalog metadata (e.g., titles, identifiers) relevant to our listings.
  • Pricing and competitive price information.
  • Listings restrictions (sellability) for our account.

Purpose of Processing

Operational analytics only: ungated checks, profitability calculations, stock planning, and maintaining compliant listings.

Security

  • Least‑privilege access and periodic access reviews.
  • Encryption in transit (TLS) and secure credential storage.
  • Incident response plan; we notify security@amazon.com for any incidents involving Amazon Information.
